2. Scope of Work

3. Payment Terms

No payment is due until Client has reviewed the completed website and provided written approval. Upon approval, full payment is due within 5 business days. If Client chooses not to proceed after reviewing the site, no payment is owed and no further obligation exists.

  • Accepted payment: e-Transfer to diazcjames@gmail.com
  • The website will not be connected to a live custom domain until payment is received in full.
  • No refunds are issued after written approval and payment have been received.
4. Approval Process
  • FJMedia will deliver a preview link (GitHub Pages) for Client review upon completion.
  • Client has 7 calendar days from the delivery of the preview link to provide written approval or revision requests.
  • If no response is received within 7 days, the project will be placed on hold and the slot may be reassigned.
  • Written approval may be provided via email, text, or any digital message confirming acceptance.
5. Revisions
  • This agreement includes 3 rounds of revisions at no additional cost.
  • A revision round is defined as a single consolidated list of changes submitted at one time.
  • Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are billed at $100 per revision round, invoiced separately.
  • Requests that change the original agreed scope are considered new work, not revisions.

  • Delays caused by missing or late content are not the responsibility of FJMedia and will extend the delivery date accordingly.
  • Client warrants that all content provided is owned by them or licensed for use.
